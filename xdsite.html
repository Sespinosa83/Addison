Hello Nice to see you here!

Addison was born in Ecuador, raised in NYC and currently resides in the countryside north of the city. In 2015 she started writing graffiti and soon was well known as the only female capable of competing with the boys in the graffiti subculture. Addison has been painting subway trains for years.
